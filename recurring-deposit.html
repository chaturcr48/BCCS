Terms and conditions

1. Opening of Account:- Account can be opened in multiples of minimum Rs.500/- and further Rs.100/-. Bihar Co-operative reserves the right to reject any application without assigning any reason. Bihar Co-operative reserves the right to associate / transfer any account with its other branch office / branch office of the Motivator.

2. Duration of the account:- The period of the account will be from 12 months to 60 months.

3. Method of depositing installments: All payments towards Bihar Co-operative will be made by depositing cash against the receipt bearing the signature of the person authorized by the Bihar Co-operative. The depositor himself will be responsible for the amount deposited by means other than the above method. Installments will be deposited regularly every month.

4. Regarding issue of passbook: - A passbook will be made available to each account holder. The entry of the deposit amount will be made by the account holder in the passbook every month. After depositing all the installments, the account holder will return the passbook to the Bihar Co-operative. If there is any difference or difference in the entry made in the passbook and the receipt signed by the Bihar Co-operative's seal and the authorized officer of the office, then the payment will be made on the basis of ledger.

5. Maturity :- At the end of the period, the account holder member will be credited to the Bihar Co-operative. will be paid along with interest.

6. Nomination of Two Heirs (Double Nominee System):- The account holder can appoint two successors as per his wish, fixing the ratio of the amount received by each successor, but for this declaration should be made at the time of opening the account.

7. (a) 75% of the deposit amount on demand of the member S.L. To be paid as loan.
(b) Members who deposit money in the account regularly after two years, in case of accidental death, three times the first year's deposit and in case of normal death, the security amount equal to the first year's deposit (maximum Rs. 2 lakh) will be given as This facility will be payable to the members up to the age of 70 years.
