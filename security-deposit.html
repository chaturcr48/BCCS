Security Deposit

1. This account will be opened only of the employee working in Bihar Co-operative.

2. Every month, 10% of the employee's salary/allowance/ commission/ incentive will be transferred to this account.

3. Interest will be deposited in this account at the end of each financial year at the rate of interest determined by the co-operative.

4. The amount deposited in this account will be paid along with interest after 6 months of the employee's resignation or leaving the job.

5. In case of loss of passbook, on a written request by the employee, a duplicate copy will be provided to the co-operative after verification and correct identification on payment of the prescribed fee to the co-operative.(Rs 500)

6. The co-operative is able to recover the same in case of interest or any other amount paid in excess.
